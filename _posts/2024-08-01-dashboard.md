You know the problem:
Winter is coming, and you need to know the temperature/humidity in your home for heating purposes.
You could buy a cheap gadget that tells you both values (boring). Or you could buy a Raspberry Pi and
an external sensor, program it to continually upload its measurements into a smart home service and
integrate that into a self-designed web app that plots current and past values and finally have a good
overview of how your home is heated. Which is what I did, and what led to this small dashboard:


See it in action at [here](https://pi-dashboard.netlify.app)
