The environment simulates a simple air hockey game where an agent controls a paddle to hit a puck towards the opponent's goal while defending its own goal.

I implemented and trained an [agent](https://github.com/magicmagnus/reflex_project) using the TD3 (Twin Delayed Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient) algorithm, which then took part in the final competition of the course against other students' agents.
